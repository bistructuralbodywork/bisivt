About Bobby

Bobby Ibarra is a board certified practitioner under the International Association of Structural Integrators. He is a graduate of the New School of Structural Integration in Laguna Beach, CA. His practice emphasizes communication between practitioner and client. You will work together to learn about your body’s unique makeup, and find your path to alignment.

“While getting people out of pain is great, facilitating growth is even better. My work is really good for people who are looking for change.”
Bobby Ibarra
