Holistic Bodywork

in Bozeman, MT

Structural Integration offers life-changing benefits for everyone, from world-class athletes to desk jockeys, grandparents, and grandkids alike.

This holistic, systematic bodywork helps people of all ages and body types feel and move more comfortably. We use manual therapy and movement education to realign the body, finding balance, relief, and long term comfort.

In a series of sessions, we will work with fascia, your body’s connective tissue, to better align its structure in relation to gravity.

Benefits include:

  • Ease of Movement

  • Improved Posture

  • Increased Energy

  • Release of Compensatory Patterns

  • Reduced Pain and Tension

  • Lasting Results

This holistic method questions mainstream assumptions on health and wellbeing, approaching healing systematically, rather than symptomatically.
